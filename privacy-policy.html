Privacy Policy

Text to Calendar - Last updated: December 2024

Privacy-First Commitment: Text to Calendar is designed with privacy as a core principle. All text processing happens on your device using Apple's secure frameworks. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. Text to Calendar operates entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

1.1 Local Device Data

  • Text Input: The text you enter for event parsing is processed locally on your device using Apple's NaturalLanguage framework
  • Calendar Events: Events are created directly in your iOS Calendar app via EventKit
  • App Preferences: Any app settings are stored locally on your device

1.2 Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization (see Google's privacy policy)
  • Apple Services: We use Apple's EventKit and NaturalLanguage frameworks, which operate according to Apple's privacy standards

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we have no data to use. The app functions entirely through:

  • Local text processing on your device
  • Direct calendar integration via iOS EventKit
  • AdMob for displaying relevant advertisements

3. Data Storage and Security

  • No Cloud Storage: We don't store your data on any servers
  • Local Processing: All text parsing happens on your device
  • iOS Security: Your device's built-in security protects your data
  • Calendar Access: Events are saved directly to your iOS Calendar app

4. Permissions and Access

4.1 Calendar Access

The app requests calendar access to create events from your parsed text. This permission is:

  • Requested only when you attempt to save an event
  • Used solely to create calendar events you explicitly confirm
  • Can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Calendars

4.2 Network Access

Limited network access is used only for:

  • Loading advertisements through AdMob
  • No user data is transmitted

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information for ad personalization
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage statistics

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy

5.2 Apple Services

We use Apple's frameworks that operate according to Apple's privacy standards:

  • EventKit: For calendar integration
  • NaturalLanguage: For text processing
  • NSDataDetector: For date/time detection

6. Children's Privacy

Text to Calendar is suitable for all ages. We do not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13.

7. Data Retention

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no data to retain. Any local app data is stored on your device and can be removed by:

  • Deleting the app
  • Clearing app data in iOS Settings

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke calendar access at any time
  • Delete the app and all associated data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
  • Request information about data handling (though we collect none)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Notify users of significant changes through app updates
  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Maintain the same privacy-first principles
